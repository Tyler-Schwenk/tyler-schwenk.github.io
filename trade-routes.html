Trade Routes

A peer-to-peer marketplace for collectible card trading, built with community and clarity in mind.

The Marketplace

Trade Routes is a California-based general partnership co-founded by Tyler Schwenk and Kyle Pereira. We built Trade Routes to help Magic: The Gathering players trade, buy, and sell cards through a modern, accessible, and community-oriented platform. With robust search tools, clear listing data, and a design focused on usability, the site aims to improve how collectors connect and transact.
