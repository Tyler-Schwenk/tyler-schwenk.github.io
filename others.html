Trade Routes.

Trade Routes is a peer-to-peer marketplace for trading cards. I am developping this with a few friends out of necessity - as there is no marketplace that allows you to buy directly from individuals with a user-friendly design for finding specific cards.

What we do different is our database designed specifically for trading card lookup. This allows the backend to know exactly which cards are in each listing, granting users the ability to search directly for the cards they need. Advanced search filtering removes the need to scroll through irrelevant lots of cards, as is necessary on other peer-to-peer marketplaces.

The Alpha version will be available to the public at the end of 2024.
